History

I was a hairdresser for 11 years and in the last 4 years in this profession I have had the opportunity to run my own beauty salon. I learned a lot from this experience, both in the coexistence and management of people, as well as the administrative responsibility itself. With the coming of the pandemic in 2020, it was impossible to maintain the salon, and I saw the chance to change careers to something I've always been interested in. I started studying and doing small projects in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and TypeScript and since then I've been taking courses, applying my learnings, including new languages and growing more and more. I really like this area and I intend to make this profession a lifelong career.
